Units Description Units Description
cm centimeters em Relative to the font-size of the element (2em means 2 times the size of the current font)
mm millimeters ex Relative to the x-height of the current font (rarely used)
in inches (1in = 96px = 2.54cm) ch Relative to the width of the "0" (zero)
px * pixels (1px = 1/96th of 1in) rem Relative to font-size of the root element
pt points (1pt = 1/72 of 1in) vw Relative to 1% of the width of the viewport*
pc picas (1pc = 12 pt) vh Relative to 1% of the height of the viewport*
vmin Relative to 1% of viewport's* smaller dimension vmax Relative to 1% of viewport's* larger dimension
